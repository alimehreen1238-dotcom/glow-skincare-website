The Truth About Vitamin C in Skincare

By Sarah Jenkins • Oct 15, 2026 • 5 min read

Vitamin C is one of the most legendary ingredients in skincare. But despite its fame, it's also one of the most misunderstood. Let's break down how this powerhouse antioxidant actually works and why you might be using it wrong.

Why Vitamin C?

It's a potent antioxidant that neutralizes free radicals—those pesky molecules from pollution and UV rays that cause premature aging. By fighting these off, Vitamin C helps keep your skin looking bright, firm, and youthful.

Vitamin C is found naturally in high concentrations in citrus fruits, but applying it topically is far more effective for your skin.

What Percentage Do You Need?

More isn't always better. Clinical studies show that a concentration between 10% and 20% is optimal for efficacy without causing unnecessary irritation. Our Radiance Vitamin C Serum uses a sweet-spot 15% concentration, stabilized with Ferulic Acid.

"Applying Vitamin C correctly is just as important as the product itself. Always apply it to dry skin in the morning, right after cleansing, and before your moisturizer."

How to Layer It

1. Cleanse
2. Tone
3. Vitamin C Serum
4. Moisturizer
5. SPF (Never skip this step!)
