Our Story

  1. Home
  2. About

Beauty Rooted in Nature, Perfected by Science.

Lumière Skin was born out of a simple desire: to create skincare that doesn't just promise results, but delivers them through clean, transparent, and luxurious formulations.

We believe that self-care is a ritual, and every product should feel like a moment of indulgence. Our team of expert formulators works tirelessly to blend the finest botanicals with cutting-edge clinical actives.

Founded in 2020 by esthetician Emma Roberts and cosmetic chemist Dr. James Lin, the brand bridges the gap between holistic wellness and dermatological science. Every formula is meticulously crafted over years of testing, ensuring it meets our uncompromising standards for efficacy and purity.

Our Philosophy

Purity

Only the highest quality, ethically sourced ingredients make it into our bottles. We trace every botanical back to its origin.

Transparency

No hidden fillers, no proprietary blends. We are completely open about what goes onto your skin and in what percentages.

Sustainability

We use recyclable glass, post-consumer recycled materials, and offset our carbon footprint to protect the planet we source from.
