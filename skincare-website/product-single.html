What's Inside?

15% THD Ascorbate (Vitamin C)

A highly stable, oil-soluble form of Vitamin C that penetrates deeper into the skin to brighten and boost collagen production without the irritation of L-ascorbic acid.

Ferulic Acid

A plant-based antioxidant that enhances the stability and efficacy of Vitamin C while protecting against environmental stressors.

Botanical Hyaluronic Acid

Derived from Cassia Angustifolia seeds, it draws moisture into the skin to plump fine lines and create a dewy finish.
